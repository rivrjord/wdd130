Calabar

Calabar, the capital of Cross River State in southeastern Nigeria, is known for its rich Efik heritage, colonial history, and natural attractions. It hosts the famous Calabar Carnival every December—Africa’s Biggest Street Party—featuring colorful parades, music, dance, and cultural displays. With sites like Tinapa Resort, Kwa Falls, and the Slave History Museum, Calabar is a top destination for both cultural and eco-tourism.
