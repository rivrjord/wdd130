History

Rafting Travel began in 2005 when a group of friends, driven by their love for whitewater and the outdoors, decided to share their passion with others. With a single raft and a dream, they launched small guided trips on their local river. Word quickly spread, and what started as a weekend hobby grew into a thriving company.

Today, we operate in multiple states and host thousands of adventurers each year. Our commitment to safety, environmental sustainability, and outstanding customer service remains at the heart of everything we do. From first-timers to thrill-seekers, Rafting Travel offers an experience for everyone.
